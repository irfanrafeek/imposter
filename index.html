How to Play

A party game of bluff and beats

  1. 1

    Gather your group

    Three to eight friends, each with their own phone. Open this page on every device.

  2. 2

    Create or join a room

    One person taps Create and shares the 4-character room code. Everyone else taps Join and types it in.

  3. 3

    Headphones on

    Each player needs their own pair. If audio leaks across the room the trick falls apart.

  4. 4

    Pick a vibe

    The host chooses a music category — 80s pop, Michael Jackson, TikTok & Reels, Malayalam hits, and more.

  5. 5

    Dance to the song

    When the round starts, a song plays in sync on every phone — except the impostor, who hears a completely different track. Everyone dances to whatever they're hearing.

  6. 6

    Catch the odd dancer

    The impostor is grooving to a different vibe, so their moves won't match the song everyone else is dancing to. Watch closely and spot who's out of sync. The host reveals the impostor at the end of the round.

FAQ

What is the Impostor Music Game?

It's a free online party game for 3–8 players. When a round starts, everyone hears the same song on their phone — except one secret impostor, who hears a completely different track. Everyone dances to what they're hearing, and the group tries to spot the impostor by whose moves don't match the song's vibe. Think Spyfall or Among Us, but with music and dancing.

Is it free to play?

Yes, completely free. There's no sign-up, no app to install, and no payment. Just open the page in any phone browser and share a room code with your friends.

How many players do I need?

At least 3 players, up to 8. One person creates the room and becomes the host; everyone else joins with the 4-character room code.

Do I need headphones?

Yes — each player needs their own headphones or earbuds. The whole game depends on the impostor hearing a different song without anyone else noticing, so audio can't leak out loud.

What kind of music does it use?

The host picks a category before each round — 80s pop, Michael Jackson, trending TikTok & Reels songs, Malayalam hits, and more. A random song from that category plays each round, with a different but similar track for the impostor.

Can I play on one device?

No — it's a real-time multiplayer game. Every player uses their own phone so each person privately hears either the crewmate song or the impostor song.

⚙️ Setup Needed

configure firebase

To enable real multiplayer, add a free Firebase project:

  1. Go to console.firebase.google.com
  2. Create a new project
  3. Build → Realtime Database → Create (test mode)
  4. Project settings → Add Web app → copy config
  5. Paste config into FIREBASE_CONFIG at the top of index.html
