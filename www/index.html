About the games

What is the Imposter Dance Challenge?

The Imposter Dance Challenge (also called the Imposter Dance Game) is a party game where every player wears headphones and dances to the same song — except one secret impostor, who hears a completely different track. The group watches the dance-off and tries to spot the impostor by their off-vibe moves. You can play it free online right here — no app, no sign-up, 3–20 players, works on any phone browser.

What is the Impostor Word Game?

The Impostor Word Game (also known as Undercover) deals everyone the same secret word — except the impostor, who only sees a vague hint. Players take turns saying one clue word each; the group tries to expose the impostor while the impostor bluffs and tries to guess the word. Free in your browser, no downloads.

FAQ

Is this the Imposter Dance Challenge from TikTok?

Yes — the Impostor Dance Game is the online version of the viral Imposter Dance Challenge. Everyone puts on headphones and dances to the same song, except one secret impostor hearing a different tune. Play it at impostorgames.com/dance.

Is there an app to download?

No download needed — both games run right in your phone's browser. Open the page, share a room code (or QR code), and play. Works on iPhone and Android instantly.

Are the games free?

Yes, completely free. No sign-up, no app, no payment. Open the page in any phone browser and share a room code with your friends.

How many players do I need?

At least 3 players, up to 20, for both games. One person creates the room and becomes the host; everyone else joins with the 4-character room code or by scanning the QR code.

What's the difference between the two games?

Same idea, different senses. In the Dance Game the impostor hears a different song and has to dance along; you need headphones. In the Word Game the impostor doesn't know the secret word and has to bluff through spoken clues; no headphones needed.
